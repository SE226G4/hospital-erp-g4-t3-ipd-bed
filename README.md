# Module Name: In-Patient Department & Bed Management
## Project: Hospital ERP System
**Module Code:** IPD-BED (Module 3)

---

## 📝 Module Overview
This module is responsible for the digital management of hospital bed inventory and patient hospitalization cycles. It ensures real-time tracking of bed statuses (Available, Occupied, Under Cleaning), prevents overcrowding, and validates that patient placement matches medical requirements based on data from the Admission module.

## 🔗 Integration Points
*How this module communicates with others:*
* **Inbound:** Receives **Patient Admission Data** and **Medical Risk Categories** from **Module 1 (Admission)** to determine ward placement.
* **Outbound:** Sends **Bed Occupancy Status** and **Discharge Notifications** to **Module 2 (Finance)** for final billing and room charges.
